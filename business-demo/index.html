About the Club

Community. Football. Opportunity.

Demo Football Club is a welcoming Australian Rules Football club committed to developing players, building friendships and creating a strong community on and off the field.

Whether you're completely new to football or returning to the game, our club provides a supportive environment where everyone has the opportunity to learn, improve and enjoy being part of a team.
