Founder's Note

Why I started Build My Website App, and why I believe every business deserves a professional online presence.

Hi, I'm Troy, the founder of Build My Website App.

I build professional websites and custom mobile apps for small businesses, restaurants, bars, and sports clubs.

I started Build My Website App because I saw too many small businesses, restaurants, bars, and sports clubs struggling with outdated websites, no online presence, or agency quotes that simply weren't affordable. Your website is often the very first impression customers have of your business, and a modern, professional website builds trust, creates credibility, and helps turn visitors into customers.

When you work with me, you'll deal directly with the person designing and building your project from start to finish. There are no salespeople, no outsourcing, and no handoffs between departments. You'll always know who's working on your project, you'll receive regular updates, and you'll get a website or app built specifically for your business rather than a generic template.

I don't just build websites for clients. I have a Bachelor of Information Technology, and I've also designed, developed, and published my own mobile apps for iPhone and Android, giving me real-world experience creating, launching, and maintaining products that are used by customers around the world.

Whether you need a professional website, a custom mobile app, or both, my goal is to deliver something that looks great, performs well, and helps your business attract more customers, build credibility, and continue growing for years to come.
